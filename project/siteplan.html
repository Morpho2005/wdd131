Weapons of Fiction

• This name shows what the site is focused on, specifically, the weapons found in fiction and why they are depicted that way.

Site Purpose

• This site will include a list of weapon types each containing a list of fictional weapons of that type, which will show, 1. how the weapon compares to its real life counterpart, 2. an explanation as to why those differances were made, and 3. a number of sources for the info of the other 2 sections.

Scenarios

• What weapons in fiction are the most realistic?

• Where can I submit information regarding a fictional weapon?

Color Schema

  • black: main background color/header color.
  • silver: text color for titles and headers.
  • blood red: color for the background of idivisual sections adds a bit more color to attract the eyes of the users.
